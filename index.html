Khanh Ngo

AI Researcher · Computer Vision · Multimodal Learning

I am a final-year undergraduate student in Information Technology (Computer Science) at Ho Chi Minh City University of Education. My research interests lie in computer vision, with a particular focus on human action recognition, multimodal learning, and video understanding. I am especially interested in developing robust and efficient AI models that connect perception and reasoning to address complex real-world scenarios.
